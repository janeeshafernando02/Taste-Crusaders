Sweets




Sweets, also known as candies or confections. Sweets are primarily characterized by its sweet taste. Sweets are typically made from a combination of sugar, flavorings, and other ingredients, resulting in a wide variety of textures, colors, and tastes.

History of Sweets




Sweets have a long history dating back thousands of years and play an important role in human culture. Its origins can be traced back to ancient civilisations, with honey being an early sweetener. During the Middle Ages, the Renaissance and the Baroque, sugar became increasingly popular. The Industrial Revolution made sweets more affordable, and the 19th century saw the emergence of iconic sweets such as chocolate bars and chewing gum. Sweets are still valued worldwide as a source of pleasure and enjoyment.

Types of Sweets

Chocolate

Chocolate made from coco beans. Chocolates are available in various forms such as bars, truffles, pralines, and filled chocolates.

Candy

There are various types of candies. We can devide andies as hard candies, soft chewy candies, gummy candies, and fruit-flavoured.

Cookies

Sweet baked treats made from dough, available in different shapes, sizes, flavors and different sweet ingredients.
